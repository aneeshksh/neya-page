1. Who we are

Neya is a free Android application designed to help daily alcohol users in India reduce their drinking through gentle reminders, savings tracking, and motivational content. The app is available in English, Hindi, and Kannada.

2. Information we collect

Neya collects the following information, which you voluntarily enter during onboarding and use of the app:

  • Your first name (used to personalise reminders)
  • Your daily alcohol spend estimate (used to calculate savings)
  • Your savings goal name and target amount
  • Your preferred reminder time
  • Your buddy's name and phone number (optional)
  • Your daily check-in responses (skipped / drank)
  • Your streak count and total days tracked
All of this information is stored exclusively on your device using Android's DataStore. It is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

3. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • We do not collect crash reports or analytics
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone
  • We do not collect any health or medical information
  • We do not require or create any user account

4. How we use your information

The information you enter is used solely to:

  • Personalise your evening reminder notification with your name and goal progress
  • Calculate and display your savings and streak on screen
  • Show you relevant motivational content in your chosen language

This processing happens entirely on your device. No information is sent to us or any external service.

5. Notifications

Neya sends you one daily reminder notification at the time you choose (default 7:00 PM). This notification is scheduled using Android's AlarmManager and fires locally from your device — it does not involve any external server or messaging service. You can disable notifications at any time through your phone's notification settings or by uninstalling the app.

6. Third-party content links

The app displays links to YouTube and Instagram videos related to alcohol awareness and motivation. When you tap one of these links, you will be taken to the YouTube or Instagram app (or your browser). At that point, YouTube's and Instagram's own privacy policies apply. Neya does not track which links you tap, and we do not receive any information from YouTube or Instagram about your viewing behaviour.

7. Children's privacy

Neya is intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used this app, please contact us and we will provide guidance on deleting locally stored data.

8. Data retention and deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Going to your phone's Settings → Apps → Neya → Clear Data
  • Or by uninstalling the app

Since we hold no data on any server, there is nothing for us to delete on our end — deletion is entirely in your control.

9. Security

Because all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, the primary security of your data is provided by your phone's own security features (screen lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For major changes, we will include a notice in the app update release notes on the Google Play Store. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, most data rights (access, portability, erasure) are exercised directly on your device as described in Section 8. If you have any questions about your rights or this policy, please contact us below.
